Tech Channel

My youTube-channel started with a simple idea: helping everyday people solve small, common technology problems in an easy and understandable way.

3-Screen Video Installation

I assisted a video artist with setting up three synchronized looping videos across three separate screens for an art exhibition. The gallery needed a solution that was easy to start up and shut down each day, without requiring technical knowledge.
The Software solution:
I used mpv combined with a simple .bat file to start all three videos simultaneously and ensure they stayed in sync.
The hardwere solution:
A PC equipped with an Nvidia 1060 graphics card, connected to three screens via DisplayPort, handled the playback.

Formatting in FAT32

This is an example of the type of practical help I offer on my channel.
Since Windows 10, formatting large USB drives to FAT32 has become unnecessarily difficult. I demonstrate a simple and reliable third-party tool that makes FAT32 formatting quick and easy for anyone.
