Tech Channel

My youTube-channel came from the idea to help common people fixing small and simple technological problems in everyday life.

3 screen video

I was assisting a video artist to start 3 videos in 3 different screens that needed to be in sync and in loop. The work was a 3 screen video installation for an art exhibition in a gallery. I was asked to make a simple solution for the gallery so they could start the video installation every day and turn it off.
The Software solution: I used the program "mpv" and a simple .bat file.
The hardwere solution: I used a PC with a graphic card "nvidia 1060" with 3 "Display Ports" connected on 3 screen.

Formatting in FAT32

This is an example of how to help people with everyday small issues. After Windows 10, it is not easy to format in FAT32, especially for pen drives with big storage. I have found a software that format very easy in FAT32.
