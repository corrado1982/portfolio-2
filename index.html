Corrado Di Rofi

Front end developer

Hi, my name is Corrado Di Rofi, I studied art in Italy, first goldsmithing in high school, then decoration at the art academy in Rome then I have studied Frontend Development on Noroff School in Norway. My curiosity lead me to investigate and experiment in different fields, like technology art and music, and often interacting across them. I have different side projects, like animations, a tech you-tube channel and a punk band here in Norway (Cargokult) where I play the drums and I have produced the first album and co-produced the second one (wich will be released soon). I have also started a recording project with my old band in Italy (EP will be on Spotify 14/11). My youTube channel it was an idea to help common people fixing small and simple technological problems of everyday life. The animation channel contain just a couple of videos and it started as a joke just to show to my small doughter that "also daddy can make a cartoon for you :D". I also promessed to myself that soon I will go back to an old project of a game controller that can be used with only one hand, so also people with particular difficoulties could play videogames. After the Front-end school I have experimented with some websites for friends and now I have this idea that i have to make a videogame similar to my favorite board game (Hotel).
