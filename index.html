Corrado Di Rofi

Front-end developer

Hi, I`m Corrado Di Rofi. I studied Decoration at the Art Academy in Rome and later specialized in Front-End Development at Noroff Higher Vocational College. Driven by curiosity, I love exploring and experimenting across different fields, especially technology, art, and music.

I work on a wide range of projects: websites, animations, a tech-focused YouTube channel, and various music projects. I`m the drummer in the punk band Cargokult, where I produced our first album, co-produced the second, and our vinyl release is planned for 2026. I also play drums in a rock band called Mordana.

My YouTube tech channel is dedicated to helping people solve simple, everyday technology problems. My animation channel began as a personal project, creating short films for my daughter.

I`ve also designed a one-hand game controller, making gaming more accessible for people with disabilities. Currently, I`m experimenting with a new video game inspired by my favorite board game, Hotel .
