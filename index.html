Corrado Di Rofi

Front end developer

My name is Corrado Di Rofi,
I have studied decoration at the art academy in Rome and front-end development at Noroff Higher Vocational College. My curiosity leads me to investigate and experiment in different fields, like technology, art and music and often interacting across them. I make different projects, like websites, animations, a tech youtube-channel and playing the drums in a punk band (Cargokult), where I have produced the first album and co-produced the second one and the vinyl is coming in 2026. I am also playing the drums in the band Mordana. My youtube-channel is an idea to help common people fixing small and simple technological problems in everyday life. The animation-channel contains videos and it started with making films for my daughter. I have also designed a game controller that can be used with only one hand, so also people with disabilities can play videogames. Now I am experimenting with a new videogame, inspired by my favourite board game “Hotel”.
